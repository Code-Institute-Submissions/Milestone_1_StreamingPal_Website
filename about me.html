ABOUT ME

Hello StreamingPals, and thank you for being here.
My name is Francesca, I am 24 and I live in Ireland.
I decided to create StreamingPal because watching tv series and movies is one of my favorite hobbies.
However, I used to spend a lot of time, browsing through the different titles,
then googling them to check the rating and then finally watching.
Besides being really time consuming and quite frustrating,
I also realized that often the ones that were rated the highest
were the ones I enjoyed the least.
And I know I am not the only one with issue
and if you seen yourself in the same situation,
this is the website that will save you.
On StreamingPal you will find real opinions on varius movies and tv series,
and you will not have to read a 10 lines long paragraph (like this one, sorryyy)
to understand what it will be about.
I will summarise it for you so that you can decide the future of your movie night in just a few minutes.
I hope this will be useful and please, feel free to let me know
in the Inspire Me section if you found a hidden gem of a movie/tv series
and you would like me to talk about it.
XOXO
Francesca
