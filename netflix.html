MONEY HEIST

×

2017
Crime / Suspense

Actors you might know

If you watched Looked Up and/or Elite you will find some well known faces.

Plot

A group of criminals are handpicked from a mysterious guy in order to plan the biggest robbery of Spain.

My opinion

I have contrasting feelings about this one. I loved the story and the characters.
I really enjoyed staying in suspense and watching a full season in 1 day, but I have to admit it has some downfalls.
80% of their decisions are really questionable and some episodes are clearly there just to fill the season.
Overall was a cool series, I really enjoyed it.
We started to watch in 2 and I finished it by myself cause my other half got too annoyed at the characters,
so I would say 50% suggested I guess.
